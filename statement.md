## Project Objective 

The primary aim is to develop an AI-based algorithm which is capable of reviewing a CV in order to provide feedback to the applicant.

The project also aims to predict potential job roles that are suitable based on the applicant’s skills detailed within their CV. It will determine the most important skill sets based on how well they match the job description, assign a numerical score to the CV and then offer recommendations as to how the applicant can further improve their CV.

The aim is to assist students in making the process of analyzing their CV easier, and providing them with guidance as to what areas they should focus upon in order to enhance their chances of obtaining employment.

----
## Problem Statement

Many students struggle to write effective resumes for job applications and they may not be able to determine if their resume includes the skills needed for the job role. Frequently, students don't have access to individual assistance and therefore the important skills are oftentimes left out. Manually checking resumes takes too much time and creates uncertainty due to the lack of a simple process for analyzing resumes and subsequently providing feedback. Because of this, a service or software program that can analyze resumes and provide improvement suggestions is necessary.

---
## Scope of the project 
• Assists individuals searching for employment and making contact potential employer with their resume.

• Determines the most relevant and useful skills an individual has, based on their resume.

• Makes predictions as to what types of employment opportunities will be available for an individual based on their skills.

• Enhances the completeness (quality) of an individual's resume to be more appealing to potential employers.

• Will be valuable in college placement or preparation for employment.

• Future enhancements will include the ability to process resumes received in PDF or Word format.

---
## Target Users

• Students in college going through placement prep.

• New College Grads looking for their first job.

• Individuals who are looking for a way to enhance their resumes.

• People trying to identify their skills and appropriate job titles.

• Anyone looking for a quick and easy way to get feedback on their resume.

---
## High Level Features 

- Will take resume input from users.
- Will clean and process resume data through standard NLP.
- Will create a prediction for an appropriate job position through the use of machine learning.
- Will identify the critical relevant skills listed on a resume.
- Will give a skills score to estimate how "strong" or "weak" the resume is.
- Will place categories (weak, average, strong) on the resumes.
- Will provide additional ideas and suggestions for improving the resume.
- Will also provide a visual representation of all results to user.
